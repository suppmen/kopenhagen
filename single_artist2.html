Per Kirkeby

Bronze

21.02.2020 - 21.06.2020

Location: Louisiana museum

Address: Gl Strandvej 13, 3050 Humlebæk

OPENING HOURS: Tue-Fri 11-22, Sat-Sun 11-18, Holidays 11-18, Monday closed

Per Kirkeby - Bronze is the spring show at Louisiana. The sculptures, small as well as monumental, take on the lead roles. And in conjunction with painting and drawing and works by Rodin and Giacometti, among others, they at the same time help to describe Kirkeby's development of the painting. Louisiana Director Poul Erik Tøjner has organized the exhibition.
